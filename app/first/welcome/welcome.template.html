Welcome to Dollar Street

Take a walk and find out how people really live

Imagine the world as a street. All houses are lined up by income, the poor living to the left and the rich to the right. Where would you live?And would your life look different than your neighbour's? Dollar Street lets you find out. And while you're exploring, we hope to change the way you look at the world. Maybe even the way you see yourself in it. Welcome to Dollar Street!

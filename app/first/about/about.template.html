About

Dollar Street is a Gapminder project free for anyone to use. Its goal is to make everyone understand how people really live. Today we feature more than 200 homes in about 50 countries. In total we have 50 000 photos and 5 000 videos from these homes. Nevertheless, we are always looking to improve our coverage, and most likely we need more homes from your own region portrayed. Do you want to join us in this task of making the rest of the world easier to understand? Welcome! Sign up here and we will contact you.
